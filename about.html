About Jashoda Engineering Ventures

Pioneering solar energy solutions in Eastern India since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Jashoda Engineering Ventures began as a small startup in Durgapur with a vision to bring sustainable energy solutions to Eastern India. Today, we've grown into a trusted name in solar installations, serving residential, commercial, and government clients across four states.

Our journey has been powered by innovation, quality craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to green energy.

Our Mission

To make solar energy accessible, affordable, and reliable for every home and business in Eastern India while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

Our Vision

To be the leading solar solutions provider in Eastern India, driving the region's transition to sustainable energy through innovation and excellence.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We're committed to solutions that benefit both our clients and the environment.

Integrity

Honest business practices and transparent dealings with all stakeholders.

Innovation

Continuous improvement through technology adoption and process refinement.

Customer Focus

Tailored solutions that meet each client's unique energy needs and budget.
